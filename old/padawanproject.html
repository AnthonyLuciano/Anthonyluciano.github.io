Project's Video Transcription

The project aims to integrate programming education into public schools for children in grades 6 through 9, with the goal of developing creativity and technological skills.
The idea is to present programming as a tool that can transform children's lives, offering them a new perspective and future opportunities.
The project seeks to connect technological knowledge with education, preparing children to become informed citizens ready for the future.
With programming, they will be able to create their own games and develop their ideas, expanding their intellectual and financial horizons.
Over the course of two months, a group of about 100 children participated in basic programming classes, using resources such as computers, the internet, and software like Python and Scratch.
The project included participatory activities and self-assessments to understand progress and identify areas for improvement.
The project addresses the need to modernize education, promoting equity between public and private schools and equipping children with essential technological skills for the 21st century..
